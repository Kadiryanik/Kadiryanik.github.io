Who am I?

I'm Kadir, I was born in 1994 in Rize. I graduated from Computer Engineering department in Karadeniz Technical University in 2018. I am doing my master's degree at the same university and department since 2019. I worked on IoT communication protocol (6TiSCH) and embedded systems at Mavi Alp between 01-2017, 06-2020.

After I quit my job at MaviAlp, I looked for a new job for about three months. During this period, I received offers from several companies, but we couldn't agree. Some companies I received an offer are Huawei, AkınRobotics, CTech and Exentech. Actually, I accepted the offer of AkınRobotics and resigned after working one day. Also Huawei's offer was pretty good. If they hadn't made an offer at the same time as AirTies, I would have accepted Huawei's offer. Finally I accepted AirTies' offer and I have been working in AirTies since 14.09.2020.

I enjoy learning new things about embedded systems, network, image processing and machine learning. The languages I have developed something on are C, C++, Java, Python, Java-Script, Go. Sequencing is such that the languages that I feel more comfortable with when writing code are in the first place.

Apart from my professional life, I like to do music, I can also perform a few instruments. My daily life is often spent in front of the computer :)
